Allow me to introduce myself

You know those moments that seem trivial, but they end up changing your life? Well, that moment for me occurred when I got my first digital camera. It was a 1.2MP Fujifilm Finepix camera. That’s right. One-point-two! That little camera came with Photoshop Elements and it changed my life.

Fast-forward a few years and I found myself in responsible for design assets in marketing departments of large companies.

Like most life-long learners, I spent some time exploring ways to expand my design knowledge and skills. UX/UI seemed like a natural step, so I decided to enroll myself in the University of Oregon UX/UI Bootcamp. The growth wasn’t always comfortable, but I could feel in my bones that it was the path I needed to be on.

My design career has been rewarding, and it just keeps getting better.
